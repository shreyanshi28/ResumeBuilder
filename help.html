How is a good Resume helpful ?

A good resume helps you in a great way to get through an interview or a recruitment process. A resume enables one to showcases his top skills, qualities and achievements. It quickly grabs the recruiters' attention, and helps the aspiring candidates to connect with the hiring manager on a personal level. Applicants can discuss their professional skills and how it relates to the position.

How to create a good Resume ?

A good Resume is the one which contains all information relevant to the job. The information should be displayed in a professional layout.The mentioned interests and skills acquired should somewhere relate with the job profile. The job experience section should list your most recent job first followed by the ones done before it.While writing the summary, keep it short and to the point. All information added in the resume should be genuine.
