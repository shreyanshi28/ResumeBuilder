Q. How is a good Resume helpful ?

A good resume helps you in a great way to get through an interview or a recruitment process. It is through the resume only that one showcases his top skills, qualities and achievements. A good resume should quickly grab the recruiters' attention by shouting out :"Hey , I am what you are looking for !" A good resume helps the aspiring candidates to connect with the recruiter or hiring manager on a personal level and is a great place to discuss the professional skills that one has acquired and how they relate to the position.

Q. How to create a good Resume ?

A good Resume is the one which contains all information relevant to the job. The information should be displayed in a professional layout.The mentioned interests and skills acquired should somewhere relate with the job profile. The job experience section should list your most recent job first followed by the ones done before it.While writing the summary, keep it short and to the point. All information added in the resume should be true and genuine.
